Whether you are an IT tech or you are just trying to filter DNS, OpenDNS is a great program and offers a lot of features to help you out. You can install it on a router or do it by a computer to computer basis, we are going to do the latter.

First off your going to visit the OpenDNS website and make an account by choosing the parental control solution. Go through the process of signing up. Then login and go to your dashboard.

Once there go to settings and click on "add network". Add the network and configure the level of security that you want. Then go into your computer router settings and set you preferred DNS to 208.67.220.220 and your alternate DNS to 208.67.222.222. And now you have filtered DNS.
